2. Core principle: data minimisation

Bitcoin AM is a non-custodial wallet without its own server. There are no user accounts and no registration. We do not collect, store or process any personal data about you on our own systems. There is no tracking, no analytics, no advertising.

3. Data on your device (never leaves it)

  • Recovery phrase (12 words / “seed”): stored only locally in the Android Keystore (encrypted). It is never transmitted and is not accessible to us.
  • Wallet data, payment history, settings: stored locally in the app.
  • Camera: used only to scan QR codes when paying. Images are not stored and not transmitted.
  • Biometrics / device PIN: Unlocking sensitive areas is verified by the Android system (BiometricPrompt / device lock). The app receives no biometric data — only a yes/no result.

4. Necessary connections to third parties

For a wallet to work, the app communicates with external infrastructure. In doing so, technical data (incl. IP address, timestamp) and wallet/transaction data may be processed by the respective providers:

  • Breez infrastructure (Breez SDK – Nodeless/Liquid): balance sync, exchange rates, swap facilitation. Provider privacy policy: breez.technology.
  • Boltz: swap execution (Lightning ↔ Liquid/on-chain). boltz.exchange.
  • Liquid and Bitcoin networks: transactions are processed on public blockchains (Liquid with confidential amounts). Blockchain data is inherently public and permanent.
  • Google Play: installation, updates and — via Google — technical diagnostics/crash data (Android Vitals). policies.google.com/privacy.

The legal basis is the performance of the wallet function you request (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) and our legitimate interest in technical operation (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

5. International transfers

Some of the services named (e.g. Google) may process data outside the EU/EEA. Please see the respective providers’ privacy notices for details.

6. Retention

Local data remains until you remove the account in the app or uninstall the app. We ourselves store nothing about you.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability and objection (Art. 15–21). As we hold no personal data about you, we generally cannot provide information about your person; your local data is under your own control. For rights vis-à-vis Breez, Boltz or Google, please contact the respective provider. You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. The authority responsible at our seat is: Der Landesbeauftragte für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit Baden-Württemberg (LfDI BW), Lautenschlagerstraße 20, 70173 Stuttgart, Germany.

8. No automated decision-making

There is no profiling and no automated individual decision-making.

9. Children

The app is not directed at children.
