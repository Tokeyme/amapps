Frequently asked questions

How do I restore a previous purchase?

In the app, tap the info icon in the top right → “Podcasts AM Pro” → “Restore Purchases”. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so you can restore them on all your devices.

What is Podcasts AM Pro?

A one-time purchase (not a subscription) that unlocks extra features: unlimited downloads (up to 5 for free), faster playback at 2.5× and 3×, automatic intro skipping, automatic downloads of new episodes, and bookmarks. Buy once, keep it for good.

What is the tip jar?

An optional way to support the app if you like it. It doesn’t unlock any features – just a thank-you.

Do I need to cancel anything?

No. Podcasts AM Pro is a one-time purchase, and tips are one-time, optional amounts – there’s no subscription to cancel.

The charts or downloads won’t load

Check your internet connection and tap “Try again”. If that doesn’t help, fully close the app and reopen it. If the problem persists, feel free to email us.
