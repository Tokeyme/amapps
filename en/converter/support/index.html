Support — Converter AM

Converter AM (called „Umrechner AM" in German) converts units and currencies and has a built-in calculator. This page helps you with questions about the app.

Contact

For questions, problems, bugs or feature requests, just email andreas_maier@me.com. I'll reply as soon as I can. It helps if you include your device and the app version (shown under the app in the App Store).

Currency conversion

For up-to-date exchange rates the app downloads the European Central Bank's daily rates, which needs an internet connection once. All other conversions and the calculator work completely offline.

Purchases & subscriptions

The app is free and fully usable. You can optionally support it with a purchase or subscription. Restore an earlier purchase with “Restore Purchase” on the purchase screen. Manage or cancel subscriptions directly with Apple: Settings ▸ [your name] ▸ Subscriptions (on the Mac, in the App Store under your account).

Privacy

The app collects no personal data. Details are in the privacy policy.
